Shooter Robots

Step into the boots of Mudrock in this thrilling third-person shooter set in a futuristic space base overrun by Wraith Robots. The gameplay revolves around survival mechanics: players must eliminate waves of enemies with limited health that cannot be replenished, making every decision critical. Developed in C++, the game features automatic doors, a health bar, dynamic animations, and sound effects for both the player and enemies. Enemy behavior is powered by Unreal Engine's AI Behavior Tree system, adding depth and challenge to their actions.

Medieval 3rd Combat Person

An open-world third-person combat game set in a medieval fantasy environment, developed in Unity. Players engage in dynamic battles against enemies using an advanced state-machine system to manage attack and defense mechanics for both the player and AI. The game also features a climbing system, allowing the player to scale structures and terrain such as houses and mountains. This prototype demonstrates the integration of complex gameplay mechanics and showcases innovative interactions in a 3D environment.

FPS Shooter

A fast-paced first-person shooter developed in Unreal Engine, where players must defeat all enemies before time runs out.
